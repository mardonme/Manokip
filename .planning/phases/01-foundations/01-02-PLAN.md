### Canonical *_translations constraint template (Pattern A from PATTERNS.md — applies to ALL translations siblings):
```ts
(t) => [
  primaryKey({ columns: [t.entityId, t.locale] }),
  uniqueIndex('<entity>_translations_locale_slug').on(t.locale, t.slug),
  index('<entity>_translations_locale_idx').on(t.locale),
  check('<entity>_translations_locale_check',
    sql`${t.locale} IN ('uz','ru','en')`),
]
```
(Drop the `uniqueIndex` on tables that don't have a `slug` column — e.g., `spec_field_translations`, `spec_field_enum_option_translations`, `product_spec_value_translations`.)
